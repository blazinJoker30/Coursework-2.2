Prep Time

15 mins

Cook Time

20 mins

Total Time

35 mins

Calories

640kcal

Servings

6 servings

Allergies

Egg, Soy

Ingredients

1x Medium Egg

1x Large Onion, Cubed

4x Spring Onion, Finely Chopped

400g Chicken, Cubed

600g Egg Noodles

5g White Pepper

15ml Dark Soy Sauce

15ml Light Soy Sauce

15ml Sesame Oil

50g Corn Starch

Method

Step-1

Mix chicken, eggs, dark soy and corn starch in a bowl until a goopy consistansy, then add mixture into a hot pan.


Step-2

Once chicken is golden brown remove from pan, then add spring/onion to pan, fry for 3-5 minutes until they loom see through.


Step-3

Add noodles and mix for 5 minutes until soft. Add light/dark soy sauce until it looks dark brown.


Step-4

Add seseme oil and chicken and stir for 2-3 minutes until combined then serve hot.
