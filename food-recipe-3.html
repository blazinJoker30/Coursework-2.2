Prep Time

15 mins

Cook Time

20 mins

Total Time

35 mins

Calories

701kcal

Servings

6 servings

Allergies

Duck

Ingredients

4x Duck Breasts

200g of Rocket, Watercress or Salad Chard (or a mixture)

1x Pomegranate

1x Small Bunch or Packet of Mint

Method

Step-1

Preheat the oven to 220 degrees celcius/gas mark 7. Heat a flameproof, overproof pan on the hob, and then sear the duck breasts, skin-side down, for a minute or so over a high heat. Turn the duck breasts over and them place in the oven for about 15 mins.


Step-2

Remove the duck breasts from the oven and sit them on a carving board while you get organised. If you wish to hold them at this stage, then take them out of the oven at about 13 minutes and double-wrap in foil, then let them sit till you need them.


Step-3

Line a meat plate or flat platter with the salad leaves. Slice each duck breast very thinly on the diagonal and lay on the salad-line dish, pouring any meat juices over them as you go.


Step-4

Halve the pomegranate, and then bash out the seeds from one half to garnish the duck slices. Squeeze some of the juice from the other half - just by hand - over the duck as well. Tear off a handful of mint leaves and then finely chop them, scattering them over the duck.
