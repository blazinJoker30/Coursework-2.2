Prep Time

5 mins

Cook Time

N/a

Total Time

5 mins

Calories

299 kcal

Servings

1 Serving

Allergies

Lactose

Ingredients

1x Peeled Banana Cut Into 4, From the Freezer

150ml Milk of Your Choice

15ml tbsp Honey

4x tsp Chocolate Ovaltime

1x tsp Camp Coffee (or 1/2 tsp instant espresso powder)

Method

Step-1

Put all the ingredients into a blender and whiz to mix.


Step-2

Pour into a glass and drink for a refreshing morning drink!
