Prep Time

10 mins

Cook Time

20 mins

Total Time

30 mins

Calories

671kcal

Servings

4 servings

Allergies

Seafood, Gluten

Ingredients

2x tsp Medium Madras Curry Powder

1/4 tsp Table salt

1x tsp Sugar

4x Salmon Fillets, Approx 200g each

30ml Garlic Oil

250g Vermicelli Noodles

100g Finely Sliced Chinese Leaf

125g Babycorm Sliced into Fine Rounds

250ml Chicken Stock

3x 15ml tbsp Soy Sauce

150g Beansprouts

4x 15ml tbsp Chopped Fresh Coriander

Method

Step-1

Mix the curry powder, salt and sugar in a wide, shallow dish and dredge the salmon in this, turning the pieces all over in the rub. Heat the oil in a heavy-based pan and cook the salmon fillets on a high heat for about 2-3 mins a side, searing the sides of the fillets too if they are very thick.


Step-2

Put the noodles into a bowl and cover with boiling water. Leave them to soak for 4 mins then drain them. Heat the oil in a wok and fry the Chinese leaf and baby corn for 2-3 mins.


Step-3

Add the curry powder to the wok, and then the chicken stock and soy sauce pour in the Chinese wine, and the drained, soaked noodles, tossing and shaking everything all together in the wok.


Step-4

Finally, stir in the beansprounts and give a final toss, before serving into a bowl and garnishing with coriander.
